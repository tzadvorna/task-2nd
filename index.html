About us

Will Wilshere is the founder of WW Tennis Coach where he has been developing players of all ages and abilities since 2004. He is a certified instructor with Tennis Ireland and the Professional Tennis Registry. Will is also a certified Director of Tennis with Tennis Ireland and has a first class Diploma in Sports Psychology from the Dublin Business School. Will is currently Head Coach and Director of Tennis at Claremont Railway Lawn Tennis Club in Sandymount, Dublin 4.
